Why Experential Learning?

Its practices have the power to model competences where the mainstream teaching techniques fail to. It develops skills that are critical in daily life, with the motto: “learning by doing”. Its methods are based on close to real-life scenarious and are built up on practical practices.

The Educational Camp Model

The educational camps we organize are based on outdoor and adventure activities, as we believe a close-to-nature outset stimulates cognitive process, making us more alert and creative.

The one-week educational camp prototype has the end-objective of developing a particular set of skills in participants. It is designed as an initiatic experience that brings the participants face to face with a set of challenges.

The challenges are built up gradually, setting up the framework for cherishing specific personal abilities. They are targeted to stimulate creativity, spontaneity as well as practical thinking and strategic organization.

What abilities do the participants gain?

Critical Thinking and Problem Solving

Group Fusion and Role Play

Decision Making and Stress Control

Survival and First-Aid

"Never Stop Exploring Yourself" Summer Camp 2013


"Never Stop Exploring!" was our first educational camp designed according with the experential learning priciples. It has been financed by the Europen Union, after being selected through the "Youth in Action" Programme, a europen programme encouraging youth initiatives.

After the great success of the camp, the participants' feedback and the results obtained in the project, we decided to patent the project as a model for further camps.

We want to give the chance to as many children as possible to benefit once of such a formative experience.
