CoderDojo-ing is Fun!


Together with ClujHUB, we started a first CoderDojo in Cluj-Napoca. CoderDojo is an international movement that aims to make programming an accessible and fun activity for kids, by creating a non-formal environment of teaching. It had a real success and it spread in many countries at a rapid pace.


We felt in love with this innovative idea and decided to start a CoderDojo club in Cluj-Napoca too! And that’s exactly what we’ve been doing the for the summer months. The children took part in the two-weekly sessions, where they got acquainted with MIT Scratch, a fantastic programming tool especially designed for children.


The practices used encourage an intuitive learning, through self-discovery and logic. The methods are adapted to the specific interests of the participants. Children are stimulated to use their creativity in designing their own projects, small applications and games.


See details about the next CoderDojo Session and the steps for enrolling a child in the programme, on our Facebook page.
