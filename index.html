Our Vision

LED stands for lifelong education. Our vision is a world where everybody has lifetime access to education, indifferent of where they live or how old they are. We believe this is important because education is power. Power to get people out of poverty, oppression, discrimination. Power to improve the present and the future of an individual or a community.


However, in many parts of the world, children still have difficulty accessing education. In other parts, some types of knowledge are not taught in schools (e.g. computing). Yet, elsewhere adults have no access to educational resources that would enable them to become independent.


Trying to be the change we want to see in the world, we work towards promoting non-formal education by organizing events, acting as mentors, and sometimes supporting learners financially.


To raise funds for our projects we sometimes rely on sponsors and other times do IT consulting.

Past Projects

Learn it Out

Outdoor Experential Learning


In collaboration with Outdoor Life, our partner, we organize educational weekends and summer camps for youth. There's so much to learn through experential non-formal methods: critical and strategic thinking, reactions in extreme situations, self-confidence and efficient communication. All these through adrenaline and nature-connected activities.

Rise up, Africa!

Educational Projects in Kenya


In collaboration with Hope Academy School from Kibera slum, Kenya, we give our support to build up a stronger and more efficient educational system in the slum community. We organize Fundraising Events to involve local community to be more responsive to less fortunate societies.

Coder Dojo

Programming for Children


We organize programming courses for children. Part of a larger international network, our local CoderDojo teaches the young participants the basic concepts of information technology and coding. Especially adapted for children, the teaching methods rely on intuitive learning, self-discovery and creativity.

Documentary Hunters

Documentary Club


We are simply in a continuous quest for discovering. So, we organize documentaries evenings and after-debates for those who are restlessly curious and want to share their latest amazing discoveries.
