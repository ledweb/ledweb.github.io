Welcome

"Omul, cat traieste, tot invatza" sais a romanian proverb. One keeps learning while he is alive. LED is a community of people that believe that life is a constant adventure in which we learn in formal and non-formal ways. We think that we, as individuals, we need to grow and expand our knowledge and domains of expertise continually.
