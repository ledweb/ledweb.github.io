Why you will love Zeeguu

1. Read the texts you like

Read any text you like and use the awesome Zeeguu plugin to access quick translations for unknown words. You don’t waste time looking up in external dictionaries

2. Save up time

Zeeguu keeps track of the words you search for and the context in which you found them. You don’t waste time writing down words

3. Get personalized language gym

Based on the words, their context, and a bit of Zeeguu magic, the Language Gym provides you with personalized exercises online and on your mobile phone

A Learner's Problems

Textbooks are boring. We want to learn a new language reading the things we are passionate about.

No tangible feedback on the evolution of language improvement

Look up in dictionary for words, but forget to come over to the meaning again and forget them. Assimilating a new word requires about 7 times exposure to it.

Do not know how often is that word used in the spoken foreign language
