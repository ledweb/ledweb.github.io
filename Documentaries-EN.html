Discover the world..

We aren't an exclusive Club. You can join if:


You are irremediably curious and tirelessly determined of discovering more. You are perpetually fascinated by the world we live in. You can't help yourself but share your latest discovery.


It sounds like incurable. So no need for quarantine, then be contagious!..


Documentary Hunters is a club dedicated to all of us who suffer from aggravated curiosity. For all of us, who would like to be in an explorer’s shoes and navigate all over the world. For all those who are never bored, because there’s yet so much to be discovered. For those who still wonder. Take part of our documentaries projections and make an incursion in the endlessly challenging world of science, astronomy, history, philosophy, anthropology. And any other subject we decide. Don’t hurry at the end, we like to share ideas and have short debates.
